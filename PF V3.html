Projects

Student club game jam event project: Two other people and myself created a video game called Mendel Mash. The point of the game is to merge plants together in garden plots to try and create different varieties of plants. We utilized Mendels inheritance laws for when players merge plants. We applied Mendels inheritance laws to the roots, leavs, and blooms of each plant. Each of these attributes have a point value and are averaged to indicate a plants rarity.
